Legal Help & Resources

If you've experienced online harassment, cyberbullying, or hate speech, here are some legal resources available in Australia:

  • eSafety Commissioner - Report cyberbullying and online abuse.
  • ReportCyber - Report serious online crimes.
  • Legal Aid (Victoria) - Get free legal advice.
  • Office of the Australian Information Commissioner - Help with privacy and data issues.
  • Lifeline Australia - Call 13 11 14 for emotional support.
